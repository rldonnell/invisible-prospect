Sequence Strategy

Narrative Arc: We know this person was exploring leadership coaching, executive development, or transformation content on thebrilliancerevolution.com. They didn't fill out a form, but we identified them through the pixel. The sequence moves from valuable stranger who knows something about you → trusted guide who has something you need → warm invitation to take the next step.

Key Constraint: These contacts were identified by pixel, not by opt-in. The tone must feel like a natural "we noticed you" outreach — warm, helpful, zero pressure. We never say "we tracked you" or reference pixel technology. Instead, we position this as curated outreach based on their interests.

Lead Magnet: Stephie's Field Manual for Busy Leaders — a free, no-registration-required resource. The "no signup needed" angle is the hook: it signals generosity and removes all friction. Since we're tracking engagement via the pixel anyway, we don't need a form gate.

Exit Condition: Recipient clicks through to download the Field Manual. On click, remove from this sequence and enroll in a post-download follow-up flow.
